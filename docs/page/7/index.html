Sandboxing and the Mac App Store

28 Jul 2012 903 words 5 minute read
As of June 2012, all apps submitted to the Mac App Store have to be sandboxed i.e. they have to be able to run in a limited environment where they cannot interfere with other apps or other parts of the system. While this is generally a good thing, there are problems when apps have legitimate reasons for requiring access to other resources. One annoyance for developers is that Apple’s apps are mostly not sandboxed.
